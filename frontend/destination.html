Jaipur, Rajasthan 25°C

Jaipur is known for its stunning palaces, vibrant bazaars, and rich history, making it a popular destination for those interested in exploring the culture and heritage of India.

Overview

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of Rajasthan. It is famous for its historic forts, royal palaces, and vibrant culture. The city offers a blend of traditional architecture and modern attractions.

Jaipur is popular for

Amer Fort, Pink City, Handicrafts, Elephant Safari, Rajasthani Cuisine.

Usual trip duration

A 2-3 day trip allows you to explore the city's iconic landmarks such as Amer Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. You can also indulge in shopping and enjoy the local cuisine.

More About Jaipur

  • Amber Fort: Majestic hilltop fort with light & sound shows.
  • Hawa Mahal: Famous pink facade palace, great for photography.
  • City Palace: Blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture with museums.
  • Jantar Mantar: UNESCO-listed astronomical observatory.

Explore markets:

  • Johari Bazaar: Jewelry, traditional sarees, handicrafts.
  • Bapu Bazaar: Leather goods, textiles, perfumes.
  • Tripolia Bazaar: Best for bangles, carpets, ironware.

Must-try cuisine:

  • Street Food: Pyaaz Kachori at Rawat Mishthan Bhandar.
  • Fine Dining: 1135 AD at Amer Fort, Suvarna Mahal.
  • Cafes: Tapri Central for chai and snacks.

  • Elephant rides at Amer Fort
  • Hot air balloon rides for a panoramic view
  • Block printing workshops
  • Traditional Rajasthani cultural shows

  • Carry sunscreen and water while sightseeing
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking in forts and bazaars
  • Respect local customs and dress modestly
  • Be cautious with personal belongings in crowded areas

  • Jaipur Literature Festival (January)
  • Elephant Festival (March)
  • Diwali celebrations across the city
  • Gangaur Festival, showcasing traditional Rajasthani customs
